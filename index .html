About Us

Our agency has been operating in the tourism market since May 2018. "VR Travel" is a dynamically and steadily developing company. We advise our clients only those hotels to which they would go to rest themselves. Travel - this is what many dream of during long hours of tedious work from 9 to 18. But even if your work or business brings you only pleasure and joy, it does not mean that you also do not dream to go on a trip. After all, traveling is a great adventure, which paints your idea of the world in new bright colors. Travels develop our feelings, awaken emotions, awaken our soul. Some people think that they do not need travel, but in fact, everyone needs travel. After trying once, everyone will understand and feel it. Entrust us the organization of your trip, the trust of customers is our goal We are able to organize the most interesting and rich tour to the most remote point of the Azerbaijan, accompanying our client from the moment of departure from Azerbaijan until the very arrival to the homeland Our activities are not limited to the offer of resort tours. The company "VR Travel" also organizes shopping tours, sea cruises, ski tours and other types of business trips and leisure. Our task is to organize a tour that will exceed the expectation of the client. A large base of resorts, hotels and extensive experience in tourism activities gives Lora-Tour Company the opportunity to choose the best conditions for rest at the lowest price for its client.
